📖 Game Manual

🎮 Controls

  • WASD – Move (fast & smooth)
  • Space – Jump / Swim up
  • 1–7 – Select block
  • Enter / Click – Break/Create
  • Mouse – Look

🐾 Animals

Cows, chickens, pigs and sheep roam the world. They avoid obstacles and wander. You can build pens!

🌲 Biomes & Caves

Grass plains, desert patches, forest clusters. Underground caves generate naturally.

💧 Water & Swimming

Place water (slot 7). Swim with Space underwater.

👤 Character Traits

Your choice affects speed, jump, and swimming.
